 Circle for  area and circumference.
 Take the radius and calculate area and circumference.
 
 Start 
 
	 Declare radius, area, circumference as Double
	 
	 Print "Enter radius of circle"
	 Input radius

	 area<-3.14*radius*radius
	 circumference<-2*3.14*radius 
	 
	 Print "Radius is ",radius
	 Print "Area is ",area
	 Print "Circumference is ",circumference
 
 End
